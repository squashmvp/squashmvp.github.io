The Garden Rules!

Thank you for your interest in The Garden. The Garden is my original approach to an artistic showcase which can be populated by anyone for any purpose be it for marketing/advertising, artistic drive, or self-representation; presented in an ambient, realistic, and animated simulated environment using original assets, music, and sound.

Basically, you want an image in The Garden? I'll put it in for my next animation. That's it. The size and image type depends on your patron tier. Depending on your tier, you get options. You get to keep it there for as long as you're a patron. There's rules. There's rules for all tiers. On top of that, each tier has its own set of rules. Check for tier info and rules just below!

Important! My contact options are limited. I will try to work with anyone as best as I can to the best of my ability considering the circumstances.

Overall Rules

  • We render in Eevee. Not Cycles
  • We use Blender 2.81 (Currently)
  • Items and renting not required to support page.
  • Default = Garden Tier 1 image with color font representative of patron tier.
  • Will default during time no image/specifications is provided to me.
  • Actors in scene will react appropriately to placed items and images.
  • Materials, photos, and textures provided to me may be scaled, simplified, or reimplemented for better performance but only when necessary.
  • Shadows are optional
  • Layering will not be considered, elements can obscure other elements.
  • You can choose the image as long as it follows the below specifications.
  • You can choose the location as long as it follows the below specifications.
  • Image and location can be changed whenever.
  • SquashMVP reserves the right to move or remove any image provided to us for any reason regardless of current patron period, patron tier, or patronage amount.
  • Choosing an Item:
    • No hateful material. (Sometimes)
    • No violence or violent footage.
    • No graphic or obscene footage.
    • Religious signs, symbols, or representations are allowed. Use discretion. Nothing heavy-handed.
    • Nothing which has the capacity to be misconstrued, misunderstood, or confused with anything that breaks any of the above rules.
    • No copyrighted images. Be original!
    • No GIFs. (Currently)
    • 3D Objects must be made up of only vertices, edges, and faces.(No gas, liquids, simulations, etc.)
    • Must not require immense effort to implement. (Will Vary)
    • No highly complex shaders.
    • Must not require constraints, keyframes, or modifiers to fully implement.
    • Must not pose a Physical, Biological, Psychological, and/or Environmental threat to The Garden, its guests and its inhabitants.
  • Location:
    • Cannot obstruct scene or camera angles of Garden. Must be placed beyond the fence of the Main Garden Area.
    • Placement cannot pose a physical threat to The Gardens inhabitants.
    • Cannot be placed directly in the center of roads.
    • Cannot obstruct walkways in an unsafe manner.
    • Must not clip through established geometry.
    • Must not directly obstruct other items.
